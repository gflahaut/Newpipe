--- categories: - player - bugs type: tutorial title: "Enable background player on Huawei devices" ---

Several media player apps like VLC and NewPipe have problems with background players on devices with Huawei's EMUI 8 interface. These problems are caused by a new battery saving policy which blocks particular background services. To make NewPipe work properly, you need to except it from the battery optimization.

That's it. If there are still problems, please leave a comment here.
